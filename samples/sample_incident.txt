SECURITY INCIDENT REPORT - INTERNAL

Date: 2026-06-08
Reported by: SOC Tier-2 Analyst

Summary:
At 02:14 UTC, our EDR flagged repeated failed RDP login attempts against the
finance jump host (10.42.7.19) from external IP 185.220.101.47. After ~3,000
attempts the account "svc_backup" was successfully authenticated. Within 12
minutes, the host initiated outbound connections to hxxps://exfil-node[.]xyz and
transferred approximately 4.2 GB of data. A scheduled task named "WinDefendUpd"
was created to re-establish the connection if killed. The file
invoice_2026.pdf.exe was dropped in C:\Users\Public and matches the hash of a
known Cobalt Strike beacon (CVE-2024-1234 exploited for privilege escalation).

Observed indicators:
- Source IP: 185.220.101.47 (Tor exit node)
- Domain: exfil-node[.]xyz
- Compromised account: svc_backup (service account, never used interactively)
- Malware: invoice_2026.pdf.exe (Cobalt Strike beacon)
- Persistence: scheduled task "WinDefendUpd"

Impact:
The finance jump host has access to the payments database and SWIFT messaging
gateway. Data exfiltration of 4.2 GB strongly suggests sensitive financial
records were taken. Lateral movement to the payments subnet is suspected but not
yet confirmed.
