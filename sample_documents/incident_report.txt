SECURITY INCIDENT REPORT - INTERNAL
Date: 2026-06-08
Reported by: SOC Tier 2 Analyst

Summary:
At 02:14 UTC, our SIEM flagged multiple failed SSH login attempts against the
production bastion host (10.0.4.21) originating from the IP 185.220.101.34,
a known Tor exit node. Within 6 minutes, a successful login occurred using the
service account "svc-deploy", which had not authenticated interactively in 90
days. Shortly after, the account ran a series of reconnaissance commands
(whoami, netstat, cat /etc/passwd) and attempted to download a payload from
hxxp://malicious-cdn[.]example/loader.sh using curl.

Observations:
- The svc-deploy account password had not been rotated since 2024.
- Outbound traffic to the CDN domain was blocked by egress filtering.
- No lateral movement to other hosts has been confirmed yet.
- The CVE-2025-1234 vulnerability in the SSH daemon is present on this host.

Impact:
Potential unauthorized access to a production server. No data exfiltration
confirmed at this time, but the host stores deployment credentials.
